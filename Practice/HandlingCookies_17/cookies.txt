Cookies :
    1. cookies is a piece of information from website and saved by your browser
    2. Cookies area way of remembering users and their interaction with site by storing information in the cookie file as key value pairs
    3. It stores the login information like username, email and password
    4. HTTP cookie is also known as a web cookie, a browser cookie or internet cookie
